THE PROCESS

From Crypto to Keys — in 4 Steps

A fully regulated pipeline that converts your crypto holdings into real estate ownership in Cyprus, through trusted financial institutions.

1

Choose Your Property

Browse Gindi Holdings' premium Cyprus developments. Select your unit, review the terms, and lock in the price.

2

Pay with Crypto on eToro

Transfer crypto (BTC, ETH, USDT, etc.) to your eToro account. eToro handles the conversion to EUR at real-time market rates.

3

EUR to Eurobank Cyprus

eToro wires the EUR directly to Eurobank Cyprus — the escrow/financing partner. Funds are held securely under Gindi's financing framework.

Once payment clears and legal formalities complete, Gindi Holdings hands over the property. Welcome to your new home in Cyprus.
